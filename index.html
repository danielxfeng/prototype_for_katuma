About Katumajärvi

Katumajärvi-info is an extension of Järvi-meriwiki, devised by the Katumajärvi protection association , which contains easily interpretable and automatically updated indicators of the state and development of Katumajärvi from the information produced by the authorities, Katumajärvi activists and ordinary citizens .

In the Katumajärvi info, you can quickly see the current water level height, surface water temperature or blue-green algae situation. You can also study the development of the lake's visibility depth, nutrient concentrations and ice cover period over several decades.

In Järvi-meriwiki, anyone can participate in making observations. Together, we make Katumajärvi info more up-to-date and comprehensive. We collect tips, guidance and answers to frequently asked questions on Katumajärvi-info's Instructions and advice page .

If you have development ideas or other considerations about Katumajärvi-info, you can write about them on the discussion page .
